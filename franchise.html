About People Group (People Group International)

People Group International orginazation is social welfare, voluntary, non-profit, non-political, non-religious, non-government registered organization.

People Group was set up on 24th December 1998 under Societies act to organize social development projects and to work to promote human rights duties environment sanitation, health hygiene, and social reforms through education training and awareness campaign. Thus, setting up new rational social order bases on good will, peace, harmony, brotherhood, and cooperation. 

We are committed to the SocioEconomic development of the most disadvantaged, deprived, backward, and neglected groups. ASK has a team of young, experienced development professionals who have demonstrated their competencies in empowering the community, especially women. They have created an effective implementation of various development projects through their transparent participation and sustainable approach. 

We aim to help the people to help themselves, as they know better their talents, problem.
